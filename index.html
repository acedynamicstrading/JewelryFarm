Our Philosophy · 私たちの哲学

Grown with the
Soul of Japan

At Jewelry Farm, every fruit is raised with the precision of a jeweler and the patience of generations. We tend our orchards through Japan's four seasons — each one leaving its mark on flavour, colour, and aroma.

Only the top 1% of each harvest earns the Jewelry Farm seal. Our graders inspect every piece by hand, measuring brix levels, skin luminosity, and structural perfection.

Our Method · 製法

The Science of
Preservation

Each fruit is harvested at the exact moment of peak Brix — then immediately transferred to our temperature-controlled drying facility. No preservatives. No added sugars. No shortcuts.

Our proprietary low-temperature air-drying method removes moisture over 48 to 72 hours, concentrating natural flavours while retaining the fruit's essential colour, aroma, and nutritional profile.

添加物不使用 — 旬の恵みをそのまま閉じ込めた製法
